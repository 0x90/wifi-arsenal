What happened to my Airport card after running KisMAC?

When you start KisMAC, it automatically loads a driver replacement for the Airport card, enabling "monitoring mode". To reload the OEM driver, simply quit KisMAC. Sometimes it also may happen, that the Aiport icon gets lost. If this should be the case, please re-enable it in System Preferences. If your Airport driver is not restarted when you quit KisMAC, then it is very likely that you had an network application running, when you started KisMAC. Please don't do this, as it usually causes problems.

Please note: Do not unload a driver when KisMAC is running! This WILL cause a crash.
